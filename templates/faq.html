Cleaning FAQ

Get answers to common questions about our cleaning services in Dallas & DFW

General Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — DFW Clean Collective is fully insured, giving you peace of mind while we care for your home or business.

When will I be charged?

You’ll only be charged after your cleaning is complete and you’re 100% satisfied with the results.

How much experience do your cleaners have?

Our team members are highly trained, background-checked, and have extensive residential and commercial cleaning experience across Dallas and DFW.

Services & Scope

Do you provide carpet cleaning or steam cleaning?

While we focus on surface and deep cleaning, we can connect you with trusted Dallas carpet cleaning partners or include vacuum-based carpet care.

Do you provide move-in and move-out cleaning?

Yes! Our Dallas move-in/move-out cleaning makes your home spotless for new occupants or inspection and helps you get your security deposit back.

Do you provide post-construction cleaning?

Yes — we offer specialized post-construction cleaning in Dallas and DFW, removing dust, debris, and fine particles so your space is ready to use.

Do you clean vacation rentals or Airbnbs?

Yes — we provide Dallas Airbnb cleaning services, including quick turnovers and fresh linens to keep guests happy.

Do you offer commercial or office cleaning?

Absolutely. From small offices to larger commercial spaces, we tailor cleaning plans to keep your workplace clean and inviting.

Do you clean baseboards, blinds, and inside appliances?

Yes — these are included in deep cleaning or available as add-ons.

Do you clean windows?

We clean interior windows and glass surfaces; for exterior windows, we can recommend trusted partners.

Do you use eco-friendly products?

Yes — upon request, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic products safe for children, pets, and allergy-sensitive clients.

Do you use chemicals or disinfectants?

We use professional-grade cleaning solutions and can provide disinfectant treatments for high-touch areas.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes — our teams arrive fully equipped with professional tools and products; you don’t need to provide anything.

Pricing & Discounts

How much does cleaning service cost in Dallas & DFW?

Pricing depends on your property size, condition, and service type — standard cleaning, deep cleaning, and specialist services are each priced accordingly.

Do you offer discounts for recurring cleanings?

Yes — weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleanings receive discounted rates.

Can I book a one-time cleaning at a recurring service discount?

Discounts are for ongoing service. If you cancel after one discounted clean, the difference is added to your final bill.

Do you offer seasonal or referral discounts?

Yes — check for seasonal promotions and referral credits toward future cleaning services.

Satisfaction & Guarantees

What happens if I’m not satisfied with my cleaning?

Your satisfaction is our priority. Contact us within 24 hours and we’ll address the issue promptly, free of charge.
