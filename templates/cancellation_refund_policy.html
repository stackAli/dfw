Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

At DFW Clean Collective, we strive to provide scheduling flexibility while maintaining fairness for our cleaners and clients.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel a booked cleaning service, please review the timing and associated fees below.

  • More than 24 hours before appointment: Cancel anytime more than 24 hours ahead — no cancellation fee.
  • 12–24 hours before appointment: A $50 cancellation fee will be charged.
  • Less than 12 hours before appointment: 50% of the total booking charge will be applied as a cancellation fee.
  • No-show / inaccessible property: If cleaners arrive and cannot access the property, a lockout or no-show fee may apply equal to a portion of the booked service.

Rescheduling

Reschedules made more than 24 hours before your service time may be made without penalty, subject to availability. Reschedules within 24 hours of service time may be treated as cancellations and subject to applicable fees above.

Refunds

Payments are generally non-refundable once the cleaning service has been completed.

If the service was not delivered as expected, contact us within 24 hours. We may consider providing a complimentary re-clean or a partial credit at our discretion.
